How to Apply for St.AJ Primary & Secondary School Application forms cost N25,000 and can be purchased from The School Office or downloaded from our website. Application form will only be sold to parents whose child meets the age requirement.

To begin the application process, Parents/Guardians are expected to create an online profile on the admission portal and then begin the application process.
APPLY FOR ADMISSION

In our diverse and ever changing world, our Boarding strives to provide a secure and stable home for students from a multitude of cultures. Our Boarding community is a vibrant and happy one, in which the sense of belonging to a wider family is very important. Boarding students learn to live, work and play in a community environment, encouraging their growth as mature and responsible citizens of the world. We promote and exemplify the general philosophy and aims of St. Augustine Jude Primary and Secondary Secondary School through its study, activity and leisure programmes. In doing so, The School Boarding operates within the mission statement of the school.

WELCOME NOTE

Thank you for visiting our website and I hope that you find it informative. I feel truly privileged to lead such a wonderful school and to have the opportunity of ensuring that each child at RiverBank Nursery and Primary receives the best possible education in their formative years. At St. Augustine Jude Primary and Secondary School, we have high expectations of our children and set challenging targets for them. Every child is encouraged to reach their full potential. We also recognise that every child is unique and has valid contributions to make. Our staff are dedicated, hardworking and experienced. We provide a safe, calm and caring atmosphere and foster an ethos of hard work based on an enjoyment of learning. We are pleased to offer a varied selection of enrichment and enhancement opportunities and possess excellent links with our diverse community. We treasure our reputation as a warm, welcoming, family school. The Staff and Board continually seek the views of parents, carer’s and visitors to our school. We recognise that parents play an important role in the education of their children. Consequently, we solicit your co-operation in making your child’s time at St. Augustine Jude enjoyable. We are fortunate to have a vibrant Parent Teacher Forum and encourage parents and carer’s to be involved in all aspects of school life or to discuss any concerns or issues with us. Please call at any time to make an appointment to see either myself or my team – all members of staff will value discussing any aspect of your child’s education and general welfare. We have aspirations for all our children that through their school journey they will fulfil their academic potential and become successful lifelong learners. In addition, we desire that our children will possess high levels of both social and emotional intelligence. This will empower them to thrive in the future as active and productive members of society.
About Us

St. Augustine Jude Primary and Secondary School is an african-based school located in the heart of Awka, Anambra State of Nigeria. the school is aimed to provide basic and essential education for student all over Africa to help in equipping them minto becoming a better person to the society and also give them the chance to be educated in a diverse, inclusive learning environment.
